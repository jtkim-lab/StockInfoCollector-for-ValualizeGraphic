Description

Yahoo! Inc. (Yahoo!) is a global technology company. Through the Company�s technology and insights, Yahoo! delivers digital content and experiences, across devices and globally. The Company provides online properties and services (Yahoo! Properties) to users, as well as a range of marketing services designed to reach and connect with those users on Yahoo! and through a distribution network of third-party entities (Affiliates). These Affiliates integrate its advertising offerings into their Websites or other offerings (those Websites and other offerings, Affiliate sites). Effective January 7, 2014, Yahoo Inc acquired Aviate. Effective January 24, 2014, Yahoo Inc acquired Cloud Party Inc. In February 2014, Yahoo! Inc announced the expansion of its Champaign, IL technology hub. Effective February 11, 2014, Yahoo! Inc acquired Wander LLC. Effective February 14, 2014, Yahoo! Inc acquired Distill. Effective May 13, 2014, the Company acquired Meh Labs Inc.
