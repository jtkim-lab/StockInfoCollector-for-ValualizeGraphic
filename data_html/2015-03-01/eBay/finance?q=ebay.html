Description

eBay Inc. is a global technology company. The Company enables commerce through three reportable segments: Marketplaces, Payments and Enterprise. The Companys Marketplaces segment includes its core global ecommerce platform, ebay.com; other localized sites around the world such as eBay.de and eBay.co.uk; related commerce platforms, including its vertical shopping websites, such as StubHub, and its classifieds websites, such as Marktplaats.nl and mobile.de; and the Companys advertising services business. The Companys Payments segment includes core payments brand PayPal, which enables individuals and businesses to securely, easily and quickly send and receive payments online and through a broad range of mobile devices. Enterprise operates in three lines of business: Commerce Technologies, Omnichannel Operations and Marketing Solutions.
