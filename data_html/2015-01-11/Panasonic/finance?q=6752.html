Description

Panasonic Corporation is a Japan-based company. The Appliance segment is engaged in the development, manufacture and sale of white goods, beauty and living appliance, healthy products, among others. The Eco Solution segment is engaged in the development, manufacture and sale of lighting fixtures, lamps, lighting devices, wiring devices, distribution panel boards, housing-related materials and equipment, PV power generation systems, storage batteries, ventilation fans. The AVC Networks segment is engaged in the development, manufacture of digital camera and mobile phone. The Automotive and Industrial System segment is engaged in the development, manufacture and sales of automotive related products, Industrial related devices, among others. The others segment is engaged in the other related business. As of March 31, 2014, the Company had 504 consolidated subsidiaries.
