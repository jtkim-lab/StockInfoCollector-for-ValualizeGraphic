Description

Lenovo Group Limited is an investment holding company. It is a personal technology company serving customers in more than 160 countries. The Company is a personal computer (PC) vendor. The Company develops, manufactures and markets personal technology products and services. Its product lines include legendary Think-branded commercial PCs and Idea-branded consumer PCs, as well as servers, workstations, and a family of mobile Internet devices, including tablets and smart phones. It operates in four geographical segments: China, Asia Pacific, Europe-Middle East-Africa and Americas.
