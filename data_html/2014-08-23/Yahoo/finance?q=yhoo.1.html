Description

Yahoo! Inc. (Yahoo!), is a global technology company. Through the Company�s technology and insights, Yahoo! delivers digital content and experiences, across devices and globally. The Company provides online properties and services (Yahoo! Properties) to users, as well as a range of marketing services designed to reach and connect with those users on Yahoo! and through a distribution network of third-party entities (Affiliates). These Affiliates integrate its advertising offerings into their Websites or other offerings (those Websites and other offerings, Affiliate sites). Its offerings to users on Yahoo! Properties fall into three categories: Communications and Communities, Search and Marketplaces, and Media. Effective July 11, 2014, Yahoo acquired RayV Inc, a Los Angeles-based provider of video streaming services. Effective August 15, 2014, IOOF Yahoo!
